Advanced Insight 1: Multi-dimensional Churn Risk Analysis
----------------------------------------------------------
Customers with 'Month-to-month' contracts and 'Fiber optic' internet have a critically high churn rate of 54.61%.
This is 19.3x higher than the churn rate of 'Two year' contract holders (2.83%).
Conclusion: The intersection of short-term billing and premium internet service is the primary vector for customer attrition.
