Advanced Insight 3: Statistical Significance (Welch's T-Test)
----------------------------------------------------------

Conclusion: The p-value is < 0.05, proving with high statistical significance that higher monthly charges directly drive customer churn. Price sensitivity is a confirmed analytical factor.
